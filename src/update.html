Keyboard Shortcuts

General Shortcuts

Alt + P: Enable / Disable Page Ruler

Toolbar Inputs

Up Key: Increase the value by 1

+ Shift: Increase the value by 10

Down Key: Decrease the value by 1

+ Shift: Decrease the value by 10

Ruler

Arrow keys: Move the ruler by 1 pixel in any direction

+ Shift: Move the ruler by 10 pixels

+ Ctrl: Expand the ruler outwards by 1 pixel

+ Shift + Ctrl: Expand the ruler outwards by 10 pixels

+ Ctrl + Alt: Shrink the ruler inwards by 1 pixel

+ Shift + Ctrl + Alt: Shrink the ruler inwards by 10 pixels
